Improves Decision Making: GK is not limited to getting good grades and making a social impact. It also helps you take an important decision in your life. For example, while taking a decision regarding career a candidate with proper knowledge of the current scenario of the world can choose a better career option.

Current affairs events can be connected to subjects like polity and governance, economy, international relations, environment, science and technology, social issues, and security. Aspirants should aim to form a holistic understanding of how these events impact these diverse subjects.

History is the story of who we are, where we come from, and can potentially reveal where we are headed. History is important to study because it is essential for all of us in understanding ourselves and the world around us. There is a history of every field and topic, from medicine, to music, to art

The primary purpose of social studies is to help young people make informed and reasoned decisions for the public good as citizens of a culturally diverse, democratic society in an interdependent world.
